Eligibility Criteria
Inclusion

The following criteria must all be fulfilled before registering for Epilepsy12

  • Is a patient in the NHS (of either an NHS Trust in England or an NHS Health Board in Wales)
  • Has had two or more seizure episodes at least 24 hours apart, OR has been diagnosed with epilepsy for another reason
  • Has been referred to a paediatric service* for a first assessment
  • If the child had a previous EEG or paediatric assessments for similar episode(s) or epilepsy prior to this initial assessment
  • Has been referred for an initial EEG**, OR a first EEG was indicated although the child was not actually referred
Exclusion

The child or young person has NOT:

  • requested to opt out
  • previously been diagnosed with epilepsy
  • previously had a paediatric first assessment or EEG for earlier seizure episodes
  • previously been registered in the Epilepsy12 audit.

* paediatric service, or a dedicated paediatric team based in A&E. They should not have been referred or assessed by an adult service/team.

** EEG for paroxysmal episodes considered as first EEG. If previous EEG was for other reasons e.g. HIE, encephalitis then this EEG would not count as first EEG
