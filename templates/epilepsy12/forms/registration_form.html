Eligibility Criteria

The following criteria must all be fulfilled before registering for Epilepsy12

  • NHS patient
  • Child must have had 2 or more seizures more than 24 hours apart
  • Child has an epilepsy diagnosis for another reason
  • If the child had a previous EEG or paediatric assessments for similar episode(s) or epilepsy prior to this initial assessment
