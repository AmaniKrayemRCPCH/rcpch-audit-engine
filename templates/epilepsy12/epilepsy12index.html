Welcome to the Epilepsy12 data platform

Epilepsy12 (The National Clinical Audit of Seizures and Epilepsies for Children and Young People) is a national clinical audit established in 2009 and has the continued aim of helping epilepsy services, and those who commission health services, to measure and improve the quality of care for children and young people with seizures and epilepsies.
